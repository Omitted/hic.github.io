Marriage Contract Procedure

1. Obtain a marriage application from the Marriage Bureau located at the Madison County Courthouse (or the county you reside in)

2. Inform the Bureau that you are getting married at the Huntsville Islamic Center.

3. Make appointment with the Huntsville Islamic Center by calling 256-721-1712 or email, after you have obtained the application (Note: it takes about three days for the application to be processed)

What to bring to the appointment:
. The marriage application
. You and the Bride to be
. Photo IDs
. Two witnesses
. The bride wali or wakeel (father, older brother or representative)
. Fee of $50.00
